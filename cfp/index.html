Call for Papers

AISTA invites the following submissions:

  • Full papers (up to 6 pages; including references): Original, unpublished results that are related to the topics of AISTA.
  • New idea papers (up to 3 pages; including references): New ideas supported by initial evidence.

We plan to organize AISTA as an one-day workshop, including a half-day keynote session and a half-day presentation of accepted papers. Note that both full papers and new idea papers will receive a full presentation and discussion slot.

AISTA focuses on the intersection of AI and Software Testing/Analysis. We welcome (but not limited to) the following topics:

  • AI for program static and dynamic analysis
  • AI for software testing
  • AI for other software engineering topics like optimization or code comprehension
  • Apply software testing techniques to AI models
  • Apply program static or dynamic analysis techniques to AI models
  • Testing/analysis AI infrastructures
  • Empirical study of related topics

Important Dates

  • May 1st. Submission Deadline
  • June 1st. Author Notifications
  • June 15th. Camera Ready
  • TBA. Workshop

Submission

Papers must conform to the ACM conference format (with the sigconf option) and in PDF. Papers will be reviewed in a double-blinded process. Papers can be submitted via EasyChair.
